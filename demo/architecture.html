Self-Improvement Loop
Approved
Save strategy to pa_memories with Titan embedding. Increment success_count on matching memories.
Rejected (up to 3x)
Analyze rejection via Claude, semantic search for similar failures, re-enter pipeline at Step 3 with fixes.
Exhausted
After 3 failed appeals, save as anti-pattern memory to prevent future repetition.
